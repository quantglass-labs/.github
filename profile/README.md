**QuantGlass** is a complete trading **academy** fused with a working **quant lab**, running entirely on your own machine. It helps you learn, test, paper-trade, journal, and review trading decisions responsibly — and every number its engine shows is one it can defend.

### What it is

- 🎓 **Academy** — 121 lessons, missions, and decision drills that grade your _process_, **localized in 20 languages** (with full right-to-left Urdu, Sindhi, Arabic, and Persian).
- 📊 **Honest quant lab** — statistically honest backtesting (train/test splits, Monte Carlo drawdowns, bias gates that are _printed, not hidden_), evidence-backed signals, and paper trading.
- 🧭 **Behavioral operating system** — plan-aware paper tickets, a decision-vs-outcome scorecard, a journal, weekly coaching, and a trading constitution enforced at the ticket.
- 🤖 **Grounded AI** — local or API models on a strict fact guard: they narrate engine facts and **cannot invent a number**. No telemetry; a loopback-only backend.

> QuantGlass is research and decision-support software — **not financial advice**, and a community preview, not a production trading product.
